What is a Case Converter?

A Case Converter is an online tool that allows you to change the case of text easily. Whether you need to convert text from lowercase to uppercase, title case, sentence case, or other formats, this tool makes the process fast and effortless.

With a simple click, you can transform your text to meet the formatting requirements of different documents, websites, or social media platforms.

How to Use Our Case Converter Tool

  1. Enter Text: Paste or type your text into the input field.
  2. Choose a Case: Select the desired case format from the options:
    • Uppercase: Converts all text to uppercase letters.
    • Lowercase: Converts all text to lowercase letters.
    • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each word.
  3. Copy & Use: Copy the converted text and paste it wherever you need.

Examples of Case Conversions

  • Uppercase: "hello world" → "HELLO WORLD"
  • Lowercase: "HELLO WORLD" → "hello world"
  • Title Case: "hello world" → "Hello World"
